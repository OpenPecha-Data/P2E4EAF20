hūṃ||vajradaṇḍovāca oṃ vajradaṇḍe hūṃ||vajrapiṅgalovāca oṃ vajrabhīṣaṇe hūṃ||vajrakrodhasamayamudrāḥ||atha

Initiation into the mandalaathatra vajramaṇḍale yathākāmakaraṇīyatayā vajrāṅku śādibhiḥ samayakarma
kṛtvā, vajradhārimudrāṃ yathāvad badhvā, brūyān “na kasyacittvayā adṛṣṭasamayastaitāḥ
samayamudrāḥ purato vaktavyāḥ, na ca rahasyabhedaḥ kartavyaḥ||”||tatastatkarmavajraṃ vajradhārimudrāyāmupari
sthāpya, yathāvatpraveśayet| praveśya tadvajraṃ tathaiva kṣipet| yatra
patati sāsya samayamudrā vaśya bhavati ā|| tayā sarvakarmāṇi karoti||tato mukhabandhaṃ
muktvā, maṇḍalaṃ yathāvad darśayitvā, samayamudrārahasyaṃ brūyāt|etāḥ samayamudrāste sarvakarmakarāḥ
śubhāḥ|mātaraśca bhaginyaśca bhāryā duhitaro'nugā|| iti||tatrāsyā hṛdayaṃ bhavati

vaśīkṛtyopabhoktavyāḥ, adharmo na bhavati| yathābhirucittaśca sarvabhujtva sādhyāḥ|
tataḥ sarvaśuddhicittatāmavetya, sarvamudrāmanasottamānyapi sarvakarmāṇi kurvantī” tyāha
